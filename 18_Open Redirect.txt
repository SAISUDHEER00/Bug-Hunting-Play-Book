                                                                                                  CVSS
CVSS v3.x: 5.4 – 6.1
Severity: Medium
Priority: P3



                                                                                                 Impact

If an Open Redirect vulnerability exists, an attacker can:
🎣 Perform phishing attacks using a trusted domain
🔐 Steal user credentials or session tokens
🔁 Abuse OAuth / SSO login flows
🦠 Redirect users to malicious or malware sites
📉 Damage brand trust and reputation




                                                                                               Remediation

✅ Implement allowlist-based redirection (trusted domains/paths only)
❌ Do not redirect to user-supplied full URLs
🔒 Use relative paths instead of absolute URLs
🔍 Validate and sanitize redirect parameters
🧾 Sign or encode redirect values (HMAC/JWT)
🚫 Reject unknown or external redirect destinations
